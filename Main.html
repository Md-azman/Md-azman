About our Music Explore

Welcome to Tech Music, Our Music Association, established by the International Music Society in Chennai, is currently the premier institution for the teaching of western classical music in the Capital. In fact, it is perhaps the only non-profit institution of its kind and magnitude in northern India. It caters to about 1400 students annually and provides instruction on musical instruments, singing, and dance. The School came into being under the direction of Er.Mohammed Azman, a musician and composer. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you.
